Animate growth

You can record a growth animation, or watch your trees grow year by year. Simply add a Solver node, and inside it add a Grove Simulate node with just 1 flush of growth. Hit play to watch your trees grow year-by-year, and see how Houdini caches the simulation.



Caching a growth animation using a Solver node.


This will only cache the simulation and, for each frame, it will still take time to build the tree geometry. For super fast playback, include the build step in the cache, simply add a Grove Build node to the Solver.
